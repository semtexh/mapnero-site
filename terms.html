1. The App

MapNero is a professional GIS mapping tool for field operations, survey work, and team coordination. It provides offline map viewing (GeoPDF, GeoTIFF, XYZ tiles), GPS track recording, coordinate conversion (UTM/MGRS/WGS84), vector layer management (Shapefile, GeoPackage, GeoJSON), and real-time team session coordination.

⚠ Field Operations Disclaimer. MapNero is a mapping aid — NOT a certified navigation system or safety-of-life device. Do not rely solely on MapNero for decisions where a navigational error could result in injury or property damage. Always verify critical coordinates against authoritative sources.
